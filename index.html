About Me

Computer Science student
from Bordj Bou Arreridj, Algeria

I'm a Computer Science student who believes great software is built at the intersection of solid engineering and real empathy for the people using it. I build full-stack web applications, explore AI systems, and care deeply about the craft behind every feature from the database query to the last frame of an animation.

I've participated in scientific and web development clubs, contributed to educational workshops, and built projects that solve real problems for real people — like 9arini, an AI-powered platform that helps Algerian students study science subjects in Arabic using grounded, exam-relevant answers.

Right now I'm deepening my knowledge of modern web technologies, distributed systems, and anything that lets me push the boundary between engineering and craft. When I'm not shipping code, I'm thinking about how to ship it better.

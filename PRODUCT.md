# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Walid, a developer building a personal portfolio. Visitors are potential employers, clients, and collaborators browsing his projects, skills, and experience.

## Product Purpose

A dark, atmospheric developer portfolio that showcases Walid's projects, skills, and craft. Success means a visitor leaves with a clear sense of who Walid is as a developer and wants to reach out.

## Brand Personality

Dark, atmospheric, personal, playful, confident. Inspired by the interactive depth of joshwcomeau.com — vibrant dark mode, playful interactive elements, and a sense that a real person built this.

## Anti-references

- Generic template portfolios — Bootstrap/MUI cookie-cutter feel
- Overly corporate or sterile — should feel personal, not like a LinkedIn profile
- Particle effects, sensory overload, noisy backgrounds

## Design Principles

1. **Practice what you preach** — code quality reflects the developer. Every pixel has intent.
2. **Show, don't tell** — projects speak through interaction and presentation, not paragraphs.
3. **Atmosphere with purpose** — dark aesthetic that serves readability, contrast, and mood, not just styling.
4. **Personal, not corporate** — feels like a human made it, not a template.
5. **Motion with restraint** — animation enhances, never distracts. Reduced motion respected.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Respects reduced motion preferences. Dark mode with proper contrast. Standard a11y best practices (focus states, keyboard navigation, semantic HTML).
